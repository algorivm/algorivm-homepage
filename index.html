How it works

Our rozii system listens to the musician and provides visual and verbal feedback on key aspects of the performance to radically improve the teaching and learning of a musical instrument.

  • The user uploads their digital sheet music manually or by taking a photo of their chosen musical score
  • This is converted into digital music notation.
  • The user then and performs the musical piece.
  • Algorivm transcribes their performance and compares it to the original sheet music.
  • Our cutting-edge Machine Learning technology then compares the user's transcribed performance to the original notation.
  • Important musical feedback and an accuracy score is then presented back to the user.
  • The system also provides a hands-free user interface and feedback, this is an invaluable aid for visually impaired users and creates a much more rewarding user experience.

Our vision

It is important to highlight that we are not trying to use Ai technology to replace the human aspect of learning or teaching an instrument, quite the opposite:

Benefits for Music Teachers

Algorivm will assist teachers to gain more insight into their students' development and therefore offer more informed guidance to help their students improve.

Benefits for Students

Music students will have a practice tool that provides detailed feedback on their performances when practising. The hands-free element of our user interface will also be a unique and fun way to interact with Algorivm making it a fun and enjoyable experience, encouraging students to practice more and with improved results.
